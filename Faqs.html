Do you have questions?

Below, you will find answers to frequently asked questions related to onboarding, offboarding, and navigating your experience as a new intern.

New Intern
Where can I get my application ID?
The application ID will be provided once you've registered for the pre-orientation attendance form.
Where should I ask if I have more concerns?
If you have additional concerns or questions, you can reach out to our onboarding team through either Discord or email. For Discord, please use the Newbies channel to connect with the team.
What should I do if I want to withdraw from my internship at your company?
Simply send us an email along with your withdrawal letter.
There's no MOA between the company and our school. What should I do?
You can request a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) from your school, and we're more than willing to sign it.
Onboarding
Can interns start with their internship without undergoing company orientation?
No, all interns must complete the company orientation program before beginning their internship duties. This ensures you understand company policies, procedures, and safety requirements.
Who is the contact person that I should put on my required documents?
Your primary contact person will be your assigned supervisor or mentor. Their contact information will be provided during the onboarding process. For urgent matters, you can also contact HR.
What should I put in the remaining hours of my daily journal #1?
In your daily journal, record your activities, tasks completed, challenges faced, skills learned, and reflections on your internship experience. Include specific details about projects and interactions.
I want to ask for an advance COC. Is it possible to request it?
Certificate of Completion (COC) requests in advance are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Please submit your request with proper justification to HR, and allow processing time for approval.
Offboarding
Who do I contact for the offboarding process?
Contact your direct supervisor and HR department to initiate the offboarding process. They will guide you through the necessary steps including equipment return and final documentation.
How do I know if I went to offboard?
The offboarding process typically begins 1-2 weeks before your internship end date. HR will notify you about scheduled offboarding meetings and required procedures to complete your internship properly.
