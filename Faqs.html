Do you have questions?

Below, you will find answers to frequently asked questions related to onboarding, offboarding, and navigating your experience as a new intern.

New Intern
Where can I get my application ID?
The application ID will be provided once you've registered for the pre-orientation attendance form.
Where should I ask if I have more concerns?
If you have additional concerns or questions, you can reach out to our onboarding team through either Discord or email. For Discord, please use the Newbies channel to connect with the team.
What should I do if I want to withdraw from my internship at your company?
Simply send us an email along with your withdrawal letter.
There's no MOA between the company and our school. What should I do?
You can request a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) from your school, and we're more than willing to sign it.
Onboarding
Can interns start with their internship without undergoing company orientation?
No, we highly recommend attending the preboarding orientation to get acquainted with our procedures and FAQs.
Who is the contact person that I should put on my required documents?
The contact person you should put is Mrs. Jonalyn Estrada, our UIP Director.
What should I put in the remaining hours of my daily journal #1?
Deduct the hours used on your first day from your total hours, and that's what you'll record in the remaining hours on your daily journal #1.
I want to ask for an advance Certificate of Completion (COC). Is it possible to request it?
To ensure fairness, please complete your internship hours at UIP before requesting an advance COC.
Offboarding
Who do I contact for the offboarding process?
The contact person is Mrs. Jonalyn Estrada. You may ping her on the offboarding channel in UIP's discord server.
How do I know if I went to offboard?
After you completed OJT hours, you may check it on your tracker account.
