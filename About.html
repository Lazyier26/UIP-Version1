ABOUT US

The Unified Internship Program (UIP), founded in 2010 as part of the Alternative Learning System (ALS), began by arranging internships for graduating students. The COVID-19 pandemic, however, necessitated a significant shift.

UIP transformed into a fully online, non-profit organization, now specializing in work-from-home internship opportunities. This allows the program to continue meeting student and institutional needs through online connections and distance learning, all overseen by a committed team.
