UIP Incorporated Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 1, 2025


We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when registering on our website.

Data Collection

When you register to join our website, we may collect the following information:
● Your name
● Your email addres
● Any other details you voluntarily provide during registration
We do not collect payment information, browsing activity, or any sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

We only use your information for:
● Registering your interest in joining or applying.
● Contacting you regarding updates, opportunities, or information related to your registration
● Improving our website and services

Sharing Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your details are only used by us to manage registrations and communicate with you.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information at any time. You may contact us using the information provided.

Contact Us
